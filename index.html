Our Culinary Journey & Ambience

Taste the Legacy: Our Chef's Story

At Manpasand Kitchen, every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and generations of culinary expertise. Our Head Chef, a maestro of Pakistani flavors, meticulously blends aromatic spices and fresh ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience. With roots deep in Pakistani culinary heritage, we bring you authentic recipes refined over decades.

We pride ourselves on serving dishes that are not just food, but a celebration of culture and taste. From the rich gravies to the succulent kebabs, each bite is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity. Join us to discover why Manpasand Kitchen is Abu Dhabi's favorite destination for true Pakistani cuisine.

Signature Menu Highlights

Mutton Karahi

A rich, slow-cooked mutton curry bursting with ginger, garlic, tomatoes, and green chilies, finished with fresh coriander. A true Pakistani classic.

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender chicken pieces, saffron, and a symphony of whole spices, creating an aromatic and flavorful masterpiece.

Seekh Kebab Platter

Juicy, finely minced lamb kebabs marinated in traditional spices, grilled to perfection, and served with mint chutney and fresh naan. Ideal for Dine-In.

Lahori Fish Fry

Crispy fried fish marinated in a secret blend of Lahori spices, offering a delightful crunch and a burst of zesty flavor.
